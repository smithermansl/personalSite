Background

Throughout college, I developed a love for coding, using HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript to bring my web and app designs to life.

Though I often devoted more time and effort to developing my applications than designing them, I began to realize that I felt confined by my limited vocabulary. I started doing simple algorithm practice to improve my skills, eventually applying to and attending the Grace Hopper Software Engineering Immersive Program.

I currently work as a teaching fellow at Grace Hopper and am seeking an opportunity where I will collaborate with and continue to learn from experienced developers, building sophisticated applications that are a reflection of my code.
